Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

You can permanently delete your account at any time. In the app, go to Profile > Settings > Account > Delete Account.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap the three dots (...) on any post or profile and select "Report". Our team reviews all reports promptly.
